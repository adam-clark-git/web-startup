Haven't played Drawdle before? Here are the rules. You have 90 seconds to draw a picture from scratch, trying to interpret the prompt as best you can. The tools and time are very limiting, so don't worry about drawing something that doesn't look great. Try to stretch your creativity, and don't stress out about it!
